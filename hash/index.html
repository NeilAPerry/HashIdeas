How this works

Type in your idea, and click submit to securely hash it! Your idea will be hashed into a string using a sha-512 hashing algorithm. You need to keep a copy of your exact idea, word for word, in order to prove that you came up with it first. The hashed string will be posted on this subreddit. When you want to prove you came up with an idea, simply re-hash the original text you hashed and find it on the subreddit!
