Hash Your Ideas
How This Sorks
Type in your idea, and click submit to securely hash it! The hashing algorithm is sha-512. Your idea will be hashed into a string, and you need to keep a copy of the exact idea, word for word, in order to prove that you came up with the first. Nobody will be able to determine the original text based on the has. The hashed string will be posted on this SubReddit.
